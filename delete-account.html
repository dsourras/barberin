Delete Account

Users can request deletion of their Barberin account and associated data either directly in the app where supported, or by contacting support using the email listed below.

Home Privacy Policy Terms

How to Delete an Account in the App

  • Open Barberin and sign in to the relevant owner or customer account.
  • Go to the menu or profile area where account settings are available.
  • Select the delete account option and follow the confirmation steps.

Alternative Request by Email

If you cannot access the app, email dsourras@gmail.com from the address associated with your account and include:

  • Your full name
  • Your account email address
  • Your shop name, if applicable
  • A short request stating that you want your account deleted

What Is Deleted

We will delete or deactivate the account and associated personal profile data linked to that account, subject to technical and legal requirements.

What May Be Retained

Some records may be retained for a limited period where necessary for legal compliance, dispute handling, fraud prevention, subscription verification, or legitimate operational recordkeeping.

Processing Time

We aim to process valid deletion requests within a reasonable time after verification of the requester.
