Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how Barberin collects, uses, stores, and protects information when owners, staff, and customers use the Barberin platform and connected customer booking apps.

Home Terms Delete Account

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, phone number, and role.
  • Shop information such as business name, address, schedules, team members, services, and settings.
  • Appointment information such as booking date, time, barber, service, notes, and appointment status.
  • Customer profile data such as name, phone number, email address, booking history, and uploaded profile photo if provided.
  • Notification-related data such as push notification tokens needed to deliver reminders and operational updates.
  • Subscription and billing metadata required to manage access and plan status. Card payment processing is handled by Google Play and Apple, not directly by Barberin.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage owner, crew, and customer accounts.
  • To operate bookings, cancellations, rescheduling, reminders, blocked dates, and availability logic.
  • To power reports, customer intelligence, financial clarity, sustainability insights, and business tools.
  • To deliver push notifications and in-app notifications regarding appointments and account activity.
  • To improve reliability, maintain platform security, and prevent abuse.
  • To manage subscriptions, trial access, restore purchases, and per-shop entitlement status.

How Data Is Stored

Barberin uses Firebase services including Firebase Authentication, Realtime Database, Cloud Functions, Cloud Storage, and Firebase Cloud Messaging to provide the service. Information is stored and processed through these services according to Google's infrastructure and security controls.

Sharing of Information

Barberin does not sell personal information. Data may be shared only when necessary to operate the platform, including with Google Play, Apple App Store, Firebase, hosting providers, and other technical service providers that support the application.

Photos and Uploaded Content

If a user uploads a profile photo or similar content, that file is stored only to support functionality inside the app experience.

Retention and Deletion

Data is retained for as long as an account or shop remains active, or as required to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Users can request deletion through the in-app deletion flow or through the support process described on the Delete Account page.

Children

Barberin is not intended as a standalone product for children under 13. If a shop provides services for children, related appointment information is processed only as part of normal customer booking and business management.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information, or request more information about how it is used.
